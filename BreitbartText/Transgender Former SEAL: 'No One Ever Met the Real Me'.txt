Transgender Former SEAL: 'No One Ever Met the Real Me'

            In an interview with CNN’s Anderson Cooper, Kristin Beck–formerly known as Chris Beck–opens up about the lifestyle he hid during his twenty years of service in the armed forces. The former Navy SEAL written a book titled Warrior Princess: A U.S. Navy SEAL’s Journey to Coming Out Transgender.
